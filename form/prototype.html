MDM Form Field Design — Prototype V1

Based on requirements analysis. Info with label, actions on focus.

Concerns
  • Multi-value vs Multi-reference — Do we need two different patterns?
  • Errors — Displayed on hover (not very accessible).
  • Cell actions — Hidden by default, shown on hover/focus; users may not discover AI, History, or Format actions.
  • Character counter — Only visible when editing; screen readers may not announce limits.
  • Validation feedback — Value errors in tooltips vs attribute errors in popover; inconsistent patterns.
